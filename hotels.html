Hotels in Bhandardara

    As we know selection of stay place at any picnic spot is a crucial part of picnic. The State's Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation (MTDC) has its resort here which offers affordable to luxury accommodations with the best facilities and an amazing view of the lake. There are a couple other resorts that offer decent lodgings in the area. The peak season sees a rush of pre-bookings and the prices are thus fixed. However, off seasons the prices are relaxed and accommodation is easily available.
